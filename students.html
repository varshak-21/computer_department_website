Student Chapter

GDGC

The official Google-backed technical community fostering coding culture and modern technology skills on campus

  • Google-backed university club at DBIT promoting Cloud Computing, Web & App Development, DSA, and Google technologies.
  • Works on peer-to-peer learning with the motto “By the students, For the students.”
  • Focuses on building coding culture and making students industry-ready with latest tech trends.
  • GDGC Website

ACM

The official computing society empowering innovation and excellence in computer science.

  • Reputed ACM student chapter under the Department of Computer Engineering.
  • Promotes leadership, professional growth, and networking in computing.
  • Hosts Teknack, a game development event where students build and publish games.
  • ACM Website

IEEE Club

Advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.

  • Professional technical society promoting innovation in electronics and computer science.
  • Focuses on standards, research, and industry-oriented learning.
  • Helps students become corporate-ready through technical activities.
  • IEEE Website

Mathematics Club

Where logic meets creativity and math becomes fun.

  • Platform to build logical thinking and intuitive problem-solving skills.
  • Promotes fun, applied learning through workshops, seminars, and competitions
  • Makes mathematics engaging, practical, and enjoyable for students.
