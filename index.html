HOD's Message

Welcome to the Department of Computer Engineering at Don Bosco Institute of Technology! At the heart of our department lies a steadfast commitment to academic excellence, innovative thinking, and holistic development.

In alignment with our vision, we continuously strive to develop an academic ecosystem that prioritizes an effective and engaging teaching-learning process.
We are proud to actively encourage undergraduate research in emerging domains of computer science, enabling our students to sharpen their problem-solving abilities while upholding professional ethics.
