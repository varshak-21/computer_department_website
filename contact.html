Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our department

How can I apply for admission to the Computer Engineering program?

Admission applications are processed through the central admissions office. Visit our admissions page or contact admissions@dbit.in for detailed information about eligibility criteria, application deadlines, and required documents.

What are the faculty office hours for student consultations?

Faculty members maintain regular office hours typically from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM on weekdays. Specific office hours for each faculty member are posted on their office doors and can be found on our faculty page.

How can industry partners collaborate with our department?

We welcome industry collaborations for research projects, internships, guest lectures, and placement opportunities. Contact our industry liaison team at industry@dbit.in to explore partnership possibilities.

Can I visit the department labs and facilities?

Lab visits can be arranged through advance booking. Please contact the department office at least 48 hours in advance to schedule a tour of our facilities including computer labs, research centers, and project spaces.
