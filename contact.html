Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Admission Cutoff?

Cutoffs vary by year based on MHT-CET/JEE scores. Typically, the closing percentile is around 92-96%. Please check the official DTE website for CAP round details.

How do I get a Railway Concession?

Concession forms are issued at the College Office (Ground Floor) between 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM. You must carry your valid College ID card.

Does the college provide Hostel facilities?

DBIT does not have an on-campus hostel. However, the administrative office provides a verified list of nearby PGs and private hostels in Vidyavihar/Kurla.

What documents are needed for admission?

You generally need your SSC/HSC marksheets, Leaving Certificate, MHT-CET/JEE Scorecard, Domicile Certificate, and Aadhar Card. (Originals + 3 sets of copies).

Can I visit the labs before admission?

Yes, we organize "Open Days" for prospective students. You can also request a guided tour by emailing cse@dbit.in in advance.

How is the Placement support?

We have a dedicated Training & Placement Cell. Major recruiters include Jio, TCS, L&T, and AWS. The average package is around 5-6 LPA, with highest going up to 16 LPA.

Is there Wi-Fi on campus?

Yes, the entire campus, including the library and canteen, is Wi-Fi enabled. Students are provided with individual login credentials.

What is the Grievance Redressal process?

Students can submit grievances via the online portal or drop a letter in the suggestion box outside the Principal's office. The committee meets monthly to resolve issues.
